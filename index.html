About Andrew


Andrew is a amateur photographer, who just recently started learning photography. He first started when he picked up his father's Canon EOS 70D DSLR camera. Ever since then, he has loved taking photos. His primary interest, has been taking photos of buildings, nature, and people. His ultimate goal is to capture the authentic beauty of people, nature, and man-made landscapes.
